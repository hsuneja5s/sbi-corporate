Our Journey

From 1806 to today — a legacy of trust and innovation

1806
The Beginning
Bank of Calcutta established, marking the birth of modern banking in India
1921
Imperial Bank of India
Amalgamation of three presidency banks to form Imperial Bank of India
1955
Birth of SBI
Imperial Bank of India nationalized to form State Bank of India
1960
Expanding Reach
Seven state-associated banks join the SBI family, expanding nationwide presence
1995
Digital Revolution Begins

2008
